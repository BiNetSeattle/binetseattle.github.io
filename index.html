We Are BiNet Seattle

BiNet Seattle is a bisexual non-profit, mixed gender, social and support organization created to foster a visible community for bisexual, pansexual, and bi-curious people, and their partners in the Puget Sound area. We support the community through educational speakings, support groups and social events. We were started in the early 1990s.

For more information about our meetings and socials check out the rest of our website. We have also included Links to other groups and resources that are either bisexual and bi friendly in nature here in the Seattle area as well as have provided links to other bi resources around the USA.

If you have any questions about BiNet Seattle or bisexuality in general, please feel free to Contact Us at BiNet Seattle.

Join our Announcements List! Every week or two, we'll send you announcements of our own events as well as other Seattle-area events that may be of interest.

If you want to interact with us online, our Facebook group is where you will find us. You can also follow us on Twitter.

Buy a BiNet Seattle t-shirt.

Your privacy is respected. Any mailings we do are sent discreetly. Our membership list is kept confidential, and is not sold, lent, given away or shared with any outside party.

Bi-Bar Bi Visibility PRIDE, Sat, June 16, 7:30pm–10:30pm

Join us for the Bi-Mo Bi-Bar: Bi Visibility Pride Edition in June! We've put in a reservation for ONE table at Optimism Brewing, but we can have more people standing and mingling no problem. Location may be adjusted if reservations are full, I will keep this event updated.

Bi-Mo Bi-Bar is a bi-monthly event for bisexuals, pansexuals, fluid, polysexuals, omnisexuals, multisexuals, & other non-monosexuals to take over a corner of a Seattle bar for conversation, drinks, dancing, flirting, or whatever!

This month's theme is special because we're doing a mini Bi Visibility Day in honor of LGBTQIA+ Pride Month!

RSVP & details at the Facebook Event.

SBWN Booth @PrideFest Cap Hill, Sat, June 23, 12–9pm

Come visit the Seattle Bisexual Women's Network booth at the Capitol Hill PrideFest on Broadway. We'll be between Thomas & John street in front of Mirch Masala & Tobacco Leaf. We'll be at the booth all day with info about our group, flyers, and other goodies! Come say hi!

SBWN is a group of self-identified adult women & genderqueer folks from diverse backgrounds working to create a feminist bisexual support network. We are focused on support & advocacy for women & genderqueer folks in the greater bi+ community of Seattle and the Pacific Northwest. For more visit: https://www.sbwn.info/

RSVP & details at the Facebook Event.

March with Bisexuals+ at Pride Seattle, Sun, June 24, 11am–3pm

Seattle Bisexual Women's Network invites you to march with us in the 2018 Seattle Pride Parade! We are THE Seattle contingent representing non-monosexual identities including: bisexual, pansexual, fluid, omisexual, multisexual, polysexual, flexible (homo/hetero/lesbi), & all non-monosexual queers with or without labels!

Visibility is one of the most important things we can do to help others know that they are not alone and they can be proud of who they are. Come be visible with us & be part of the change we create with pride!
