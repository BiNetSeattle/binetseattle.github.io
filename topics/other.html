Other Seattle Area Bi Meetings

Seattle Bisexual Women's Network (SBWN)

Seeks to provide a safe place for bi women to explore all aspects of their sexual orientation and identity. All self identified women are welcome to attend their activities. You can find additional information about SBWN on the Seattle Bisexual Women's Network website.

Seattle Married Men's Group (SMMG)

A monthly support group for married, gay/bisexual men in the Puget Sound. This group meets every 3rd Thursday of the month. Meetings start at 7:30 pm at Group Health on 15th Avenue. The groups goal is to support gay/bi men in their marriages and to provide a place where they can discuss the issues that affect them and their wives. For more information about SMMG contact: Gay Father's Association of Seattle
